How it works

From inquiry to deployment

Every inquiry follows a structured deployment pathway so your hospital needs are reviewed, scoped, and activated without delays.

01

Submit your request

Share your hospital size, department needs, module requirements, and timeline. Attach current system details if available — our solutions team reviews within one business day.

02

Solutions review

A solutions specialist assesses requirements, matches you with the right modules, and confirms deployment scope. You receive a scoped proposal with options and next steps.

03

System deployment

Our deployment team finalizes your hospital setup — admission, billing, bed management, or full modules. Everything syncs to your admin dashboard with training and ongoing support.
