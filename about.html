Our story

One platform. One patient record. A vision for continuous care.

In 2020, Dr. Elena Vasquez founded Stackly Hospital Management in a Bangalore health-tech incubator with a secure patient records system, a small hospital network, and a conviction that digital hospital management could match manual operations—without the delays and fragmented records that frustrate staff and administrators alike.

Small hospitals adopted our admission module for faster patient intake. Growing facilities needed bed management integration. Regional hospital networks required coordinated billing across departments. Within five years we scaled to 180 staff users, 50,000+ patients managed, and deployments across Karnataka and neighboring states—without losing the operational rigor and encrypted-record standard that defined our first deployment.

Every admission is documented, every record is encrypted at rest and in transit, and every billing entry is verified by authorized staff before it reaches your hospital dashboard.
  • Trained staff on every admission and billing module
  • HIPAA-ready infrastructure with role-based access and audit logs
  • 50,000+ patients managed across admissions, billing, and scheduling

Philosophy

Three pillars. One care network.

Every decision in our platform and at the bedside flows from principles we refuse to compromise on.

01

Patient-first

Shorter waits, clear visit prep, and portal follow-up designed around real patient journeys—not administrative convenience. Care should feel continuous, not transactional.

02

Clinical precision

Evidence-based workflows, trained staff oversight, and bed allocation rules calibrated to each department. We don't deliver hospital processes we wouldn't trust for our own facilities.

03

Connected care

Admissions, bed management, billing, and scheduling unified on one encrypted timeline—so every member of the hospital team sees the same picture without switching systems.

Our journey

Six years of milestones

From a single hospital pilot to a regional hospital network—every chapter shaped by operational precision and patient trust.

2020

Founded in Bangalore

Dr. Elena Vasquez launches Stackly Hospital Management with a HIPAA-ready patient records system and digital admissions for hospitals in the Bangalore metro.

2021

Launched bed management

Added real-time bed tracking for wards and ICU—first 5,000 beds managed with administrator-led occupancy alerts.
